Leah is a member of the Ember.js Core Team and an organizer of numerous annual tech events, like RustConf, EmberConf and RailsConf. She's worked with open source projects (like jQuery, Bundler and Merb) to help build communities and events. She has a background in startup marketing in community-oriented engineering environments, and is CEO at Tilde Inc. She is the author of Event Driven, the guide to running successful tech events.
